About me

Born and raised in the shadows of the everlasting hills I’m fueled by peanut butter, good tunes, and a love for making and creating. My career path started with me teaching a middle school class called Exploring Technology. Not long into teaching I realized I'd rather be designing technologies than teaching about them. I didn't know anything about UX at the time, all I knew was I was fascinated with the concepts that human centered design promotes and that I crave being creative and creating things. Eventually I found UX, decided to quit my teaching job and give this new career path all I have.
